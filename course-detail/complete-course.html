About this course

Master Stock Trading from Beginner to Advanced Level.

The Complete Stock Market Course by Stock Uncle is designed for traders of all levels. Whether you are a beginner looking to understand the stock market or an experienced trader wanting to refine your strategies, this course will guide you step by step.

This course combines Foundational Learning (Basics, Chart Analysis, Market Behavior, and Trading Types) with Advanced Strategies (Smart Money Concepts, Derivatives, Risk Management, and Trading Psychology). You will learn technical and fundamental analysis, master trading strategies, and get live market experience through real-time trading sessions.

By the end of this course, you will have the knowledge, confidence, and skills to trade smartly and profitably in the stock market.

Market Basics

Learn the fundamentals of the stock market, including how stocks, indices, and exchanges work. Understand key terms like bull & bear markets, IPOs, and market capitalization to start your trading journey with confidence.

Chart Analysis

Master the art of reading stock charts using candlestick patterns, support & resistance levels, and indicators like RSI and Moving Averages. This will help you predict price movements and make better trading decisions.

Market Behavior

Understand how market trends, news, and economic factors impact stock prices. Learn to analyze market psychology and investor sentiment to identify the best opportunities for buying and selling.

Types of Trading (Swing Trading, Day Trading, Scalping)

Explore different trading styles and find what suits you best:

  • Swing Trading – Holding stocks for a few days to weeks for profit.
  • Day Trading – Buying and selling stocks within a single trading day.
  • Scalping – Making multiple small trades for quick profits in minutes.

Trading Strategies

Having the right trading strategy is key to success in the stock market. In this section, you will learn different strategies used by expert traders.

  • Learn profitable trading strategies for different market conditions.
  • Discover entry and exit points for smart trading.
  • Learn how to minimize risks while maximizing profits.

Live Session

Get hands-on experience with real-time trading in our live sessions. Watch expert traders analyze the market, make trades, and explain their strategies in action.

Smart Money Concept (SMC)

The Smart Money Concept (SMC) helps traders understand how big institutional investors (banks, hedge funds, and market makers) operate in the market. Instead of following retail trading strategies, you will learn how to track smart money movements and trade alongside them for better success.

  • Learn how institutions manipulate stock prices.
  • Identify liquidity zones and smart money footprints.
  • Understand order blocks, Wyckoff theory, and supply & demand zones.
  • Spot stop-loss hunting and avoid retail trader traps.

Derivatives (Futures & Options - F&O)

Derivatives trading, including Futures & Options (F&O), allows traders to maximize profits with leverage. In this section, you will learn how to trade derivatives safely and effectively.

  • Understand the basics of Futures & Options (F&O) trading.
  • Learn about option chain analysis, open interest (OI), and Greeks.
  • Explore hedging strategies to minimize risks.
  • Master different F&O trading strategies used by professionals.

Risk and Money Management

One of the biggest reasons traders fail in the stock market is poor risk and money management. In this section, you will learn how to protect your capital while maximizing profits.

  • Learn the golden rules of risk management in trading.
  • Set the right stop-loss and take-profit levels.
  • Understand position sizing to avoid big losses.
  • Create a trading plan to stay disciplined and consistent.

Trading Psychology + Live Session

Emotions play a major role in trading. This section teaches you how to stay calm and make rational decisions, even in volatile market conditions.

  • Understand fear, greed, and FOMO in trading.
  • Develop a winning mindset and emotional control.
  • Learn how to stick to your trading strategy without overtrading.
  • Experience live trading sessions to apply psychology in real-time.
