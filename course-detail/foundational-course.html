About this course

The Foundational Course by Stock Uncle is designed to help beginners build a strong understanding of the stock market. This course covers everything from market basics to chart analysis, trading strategies, and live sessions. Whether you are new to investing or looking to sharpen your skills, this course will give you the knowledge and confidence to trade smartly.

What will you learn?

  • Market Basics
  • Chart Analysis
  • Market Behavior
  • Types of Trading (Swing Trading, Day Trading, Scalping)
  • Live Session

Market Basics

Learn the fundamentals of the stock market, including how stocks, indices, and exchanges work. Understand key terms like bull & bear markets, IPOs, and market capitalization to start your trading journey with confidence.

Chart Analysis

Master the art of reading stock charts using candlestick patterns, support & resistance levels, and indicators like RSI and Moving Averages. This will help you predict price movements and make better trading decisions.

Market Behavior

Understand how market trends, news, and economic factors impact stock prices. Learn to analyze market psychology and investor sentiment to identify the best opportunities for buying and selling.

Types of Trading (Swing Trading, Day Trading, Scalping)

Explore different trading styles and find what suits you best:

  • Swing Trading – Holding stocks for a few days to weeks for profit.
  • Day Trading – Buying and selling stocks within a single trading day.
  • Scalping – Making multiple small trades for quick profits in minutes.

Live Session

Get hands-on experience with real-time trading in our live sessions. Watch expert traders analyze the market, make trades, and explain their strategies in action.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Beginners who want to learn stock trading.
  • Strong Market Foundation – Learn the basics of stock trading and investing.
  • Easy-to-Understand Concepts – Simple explanations for beginners.
  • Master Chart Analysis – Understand price movements, candlesticks, and patterns.
  • Learn Market Behavior – Identify trends, support, and resistance levels.
  • Risk Management Skills – Protect your capital and minimize losses.
  • Practical Learning Approach – Apply concepts directly to the stock market.
  • Foundation for Advanced Strategies – Build a base for learning Smart Money Concepts, Futures & Options, and other pro-level strategies.

Benefits of the course

  • Learn stock market basics in native language
  • Investors looking to understand market behavior.
  • Anyone who wants to start trading with confidence.
